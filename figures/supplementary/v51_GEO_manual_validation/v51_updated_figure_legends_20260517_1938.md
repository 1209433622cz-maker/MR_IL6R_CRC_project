# v51 updated figure and supplementary figure legends

## Figure 6. Single-cell IL6R and IL6-axis context in colorectal cancer immune microenvironments

(A) UMAP representation of the GSE146771 single-cell RNA-seq source table, coloured by global immune cell class. (B) IL6R expression projected onto the same single-cell embedding. (C) Fraction of IL6R-positive cells by global cell class. (D) Median IL6-axis module score by global cell class. The v51 candidate was regenerated from the v50 Figure 6 cell-level source table and is intended to provide a cleaner expression-focused replacement for the previous more crowded single-cell panel.

## Supplementary Figure S2. Manual GEO phenotype validation of IL6R expression

(A) GSE39582 tumour-normal comparison showing higher IL6R expression in non-tumoral colorectal mucosa than in tumour samples. (B) Stage-stratified GEO validation comparing early-stage and advanced-stage tumours across GSE14333, GSE17536, GSE17537 and GSE39582. (C) Univariable Cox models for available OS, DFS/RFS and DSS endpoints in GSE17536, GSE17537 and GSE39582. These analyses support a conservative interpretation in which IL6R is not a robust standalone stage or survival biomarker.
