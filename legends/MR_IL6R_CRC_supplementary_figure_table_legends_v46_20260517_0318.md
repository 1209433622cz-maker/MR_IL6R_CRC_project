## Supplementary Figure S1. External GEO validation of IL6R expression and prognostic associations

(A) Extraction status of external GEO CRC validation cohorts. IL6R expression was successfully extracted from GSE17536, GSE17537 and GSE14333, while GSE39582 could not be parsed in the current automated run because the GEO/GPL download timed out. (B) Distribution of primary-probe IL6R expression across the successfully extracted cohorts. (C) Probe-level IL6R expression audit across matched GPL570 IL6R probes. (D) Univariable Cox proportional-hazards validation of z-scored IL6R expression in cohorts with available survival metadata. No OS, DFS or DSS model showed a significant association. (E) Interpretation summary. The GEO validation supports the conclusion that IL6R is not a robust standalone prognostic marker, while tumor-normal validation was not claimed because the extracted GEO metadata did not support a reliable automated tumor/normal split.
